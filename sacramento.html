Capitol Building

Photo from State Capitol Building

The California Capitol can be found in downtown Sacramento. Public tours are offered on weekdays and are a great way of soaking in the history of the Capitol building.

The city was incorporated in 1849 and is the oldest incorporated city in California.(City Government, City of Sacramento)
