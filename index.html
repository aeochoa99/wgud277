State Trees


Photo by Kiel Maddox

The Giant Sequoias pictured above are one of the state trees of California. They are the largest trees in the world according to the National Park Service. The other state tree of California is the Coast Redwood. The General Sherman Tree is the largest tree in the world and is located in the Giant Forest of Sequoia National Park.
