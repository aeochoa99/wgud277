City Hall

Photo from Los Angeles Conservancy

Los Angeles, nicknamed the City of Angels, has four City Hall locations. They are located in Downtown, San Fernando Valley, San Pedro, and Westside.

Los Angeles was incorporated on April 4th, 1850.

(The City of Los Angeles History, Bureau of Street Services)
